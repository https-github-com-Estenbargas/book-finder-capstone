UseCase/InteractionFlow

User Story

As a first time user, I want to know how to use a book finding site which is easy to understand.

UseCase

Grant was looking for a website where he could find a new book, in the process his friend Paula the librarian recommended the website to him, so he decided to check out the website.

Conditionals

  • Pre conditional: Grant is on the sign-up page.
  • Post conditional: Grant finishes the signup and is on the home screen.

Interaction Flow

  • Grant looks at the signup information form and clicks on Email section.
  • The website selects the email section of the form.
  • Grant types in a invalid email.
  • The website creates a popup which states the email is invalid - Check for Spelling.
  • Grant erases his text and retypes in a valid email.
  • Grant then selects the username section of the form.
  • The website selects the username section of the form.
  • Grant types a username.
  • The website says the username is not taken.
  • Grant then clicks on the password section of the form.
  • The website selects the password section of the form.
  • Grant then types in a password.
  • The website then indicates that his password is less than the requirement.
  • Grant erases his password and retypes it into a valid password.
  • The website activates the signup button at the bottom of the form.
  • Grant clicks on the button.
  • The Website creates grants accounts. In the process it also redirects him to the homepage.
