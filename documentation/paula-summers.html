Paula Summers usecase interactionflow

Usecase

Paula wants to search for new books to add to her library, so she logs into our site.

Conditionals

Pre-conditional: Paula has logged into our site, and is currently on the Home page.

Post-conditional: Paula found an interesting book and saved it to her library.

Interaction Flow

  • She clicks on the search bar which is located on top of the sidebar.
  • The website selects the search bar
  • She types in the search bar
  • The website brings up a selection of books with similar titles in a separate popup menu.
  • Paula notices the selections on the popup menu and clicks on one of the selections.
  • The website closes the popup and expands the book's information over the previous rows.
  • Paula would look over the information, and she decides she didn't like the book, so she clicks the browse section.
  • The website replaces the expanded information and redirects to the browse section.
  • Paula looks through the browse section and finds a book she likes, so she clicks on the selection.
  • The website brings her to the selection that she made and expands the information.
  • Paula looks over the information and decides to bookmark the book, so she clicks on the bookmark dropdown in the top right of the information.
  • The website then automatically saves her selection in her library.

User Story

User Story: As a casual user, I want a fast and efficient way to find and save books.
